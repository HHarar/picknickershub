Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

Picknickers Hub is a static website hosted on GitHub Pages. We do not operate servers, collect personal data, or use tracking cookies by default. This policy explains what limited data interactions occur when you use the site.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not require account registration.
  • We do not use analytics services (e.g., Google Analytics) unless explicitly added.
  • We do not sell or share personal information with third parties.
  • We do not store cookies beyond what the browser requires for session functionality.

GitHub Pages Hosting

The site is hosted by GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect basic server logs (IP address, browser type, referring URL) as part of their infrastructure. Please review GitHub's Privacy Statement for details.

Notification Sign-Up (Optional)

If you submit your email via our "Notify Me" form, that email is handled by a third-party form service (e.g., Formspree). We use it solely to send you notifications about new games. You can unsubscribe at any time via the link in any email we send.

Local Storage

Some games may store game state (scores, preferences) in your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device and can be cleared via your browser settings.

Contact

For privacy questions, open an issue on our GitHub repository.

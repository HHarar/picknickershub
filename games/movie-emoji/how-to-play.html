Movie Emoji Guessing Game

Decode movie titles hidden in emoji clues — the more you know, the more you score. Great for game night with 2 teams or up to 16 players.

The Basics

What is it?

The host shows a set of emojis on the TV screen. They're clues to a movie title. Players shout out their guesses — or type them from their phones. Harder questions also ask for the lead actor and a famous quote or song. Points are awarded per component, so partial credit always counts.

👥 2–16 players ⏱️ 20–45 min 📺 Needs a TV or projector 🇮🇳 70% Bollywood · 🌍 30% Hollywood 🎲 Manual or Random playlist

Difficulty Levels

Three Categories

The host picks the category before each round. Partial credit applies for Medium and Hard — you can earn points even if you don't get every component right.

🟢
Easy
10 points total

Identify the movie from the emoji clue. No extra knowledge needed — just the title.

🎬 Movie title 10 pts
Example: 🚂 🌻 💍 → Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge
🟡
Medium
20 points total

Name the movie AND the lead actor or actress. Each component is worth points independently — so a half-answer still scores.

🎬 Movie title 10 pts
🎭 Lead actor / actress 10 pts
Example: 🛳️ 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 🌊 → Dil Dhadakne Do / Ranveer Singh & Priyanka Chopra
🔴
Hard
30 points total

Name the movie, the lead actor, AND recite a famous quote or name a famous song from the film. Each component scored separately.

🎬 Movie title 10 pts
🎭 Lead actor / actress 10 pts
💬 Famous quote or song 10 pts
Example: 🧱 🤵 🆚 → Deewar / Amitabh Bachchan / "Mere paas maa hai"

Step-by-Step

How to Play

One person is the host — they control everything from their laptop. Everyone else is a player — they watch the TV and shout (or type) their answers.

🎮
Host Guide
Running the game from your laptop
1
Open host.html on your laptop. This is your private control panel — the TV will never show it.
2
Configure the game: choose Teams or Individual, enter names, pick your movie origin (Bollywood / Hollywood / All), pick genres, and choose Manual or Random question mode.
3
Click Start Game, then click 📺 Open TV View. A new browser window opens — drag it to your TV or projector and click it to go fullscreen.
4
Choose a category tab (Easy / Medium / Hard), then click any emoji card to preview the question. You see the full answer — players don't.
5
Click 📺 Show on TV to push the emoji clue to the big screen. Optionally start the timer for time pressure.
6
Listen to players' answers, then click Reveal Title → Reveal Actor → Reveal Quote to unveil each component one at a time.
7
Use the award point buttons to give points to the correct team or player for each component. Points update live on the TV scoreboard.
8
Click Next → to clear the screen and move to the next question. In Random mode it auto-advances through your playlist.
9
When done, click End Game to show the final winner on the TV.
💡 Pro tip: Use the 🎲 Random Pick button to instantly grab an unused question without scanning the grid — great for keeping the game pace up.
📱
Player Guide
Playing from the couch (or your phone)
1
Gather around the TV. The host will display an emoji combination — that's your clue.
2
Check the category badge on screen. It tells you exactly what you need to answer — title only (Easy), or title + actor (Medium), or all three (Hard).
3
Shout out your answer before or after the timer runs out, OR type it on your phone at player.html?room=XXXXX (room code shown on screen).
4
The host reveals components one at a time. You can change or add to your answer until the host clicks Reveal.
5
Points are awarded per component — partial credit counts! Getting the movie right but not the actor still earns you points.
6
Watch the scoreboard at the bottom of the TV screen update in real time after each question.
7
The player or team with the most points when the last question is played wins!
💡 Phone play: Open your browser, go to the game URL, and add /player.html?room=XXXXX with the room code shown on the TV. Submit answers without leaving your seat.

Game Night Tips

Make it More Fun

🎲
Use Random Mode
Let the game auto-shuffle a playlist of 15–20 questions across all difficulties. No prep needed — just start and play.
🇮🇳
Bollywood Nights
Filter to Bollywood only for a desi-themed game night. Mix genres or focus on Romance or Action for a themed round.
⏱️
Add Time Pressure
Enable the 30-second timer for chaotic, fast-paced rounds. Great for Easy questions when everyone knows the films.
🏆
Couples vs Couples
8 couples? Split into 2 teams of 4 couples each. One spokesperson from each team submits the answer — keeps it competitive.
🎭
Act It Out
House rule: for Hard questions, players can act out the movie scene instead of saying the quote — host decides if it counts.
💬
Quote It Loud
For Hard questions, require players to say the quote in character, with the accent. Wrong accent = no points. (Unofficial rule, highly recommended.)
